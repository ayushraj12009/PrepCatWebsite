Welcome to PrepCAT


Live small group (only 10 students per batch) quants class for CAT (Common Admission Test), GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test) & Other Management Exams

The CAT & GMAT are one of the most competitive and highly sought after MBA entrance exams in India, and success in this test requires a strong foundation in quantitative reasoning and analysis. We understand that quants can be a challenging subject for many students, and that's why we are here. We are dedicated to help aspirants excel in the Quantitative Aptitude section of the exam.

Our live classes are designed to help students understand the underlying concepts thoroughly. We provide regular live classes, assignments, DPP, doubt & discussion sessions, helping students build confidence and improve their problem-solving skills. Along with regular classes, we also provide personalized feedback to help students track their progress and identify areas for improvement.
